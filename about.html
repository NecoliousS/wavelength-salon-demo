Our Story

Wavelength SMS was founded with a simple mission: make business communication human again. In a world of chatbots and automated phone trees, we believe in the power of a simple text message.

Started in 2020, we've grown from a two-person startup to serving thousands of businesses worldwide. But our core belief remains the same — technology should enhance human connection, not replace it.
