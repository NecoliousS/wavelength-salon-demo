About Us

"Dramatic Results, No Drama"

Our Story

We are a family of hair stylists, Jackson natives, and most importantly, small business owners proudly serving our community for more than 30 years.

Owners Paul and Gale Fisher founded Wave Lengths Salon in 1982. Their dedication to quality and excellent customer service are attributes they passed on to their children, Shane and Destin, who both joined the salon in 2006 and 2007.

"Our philosophy is simple – you deserve to look your best on your schedule, without giving up hours of your day."

Our Philosophy

We believe our clients deserve to get the look they want the first time—whether trendy or timeless—with attentive care from a stylist who knows what they're doing. We pride ourselves on providing our clients with a welcoming atmosphere where they can escape mainstream salon antics and truly relax.

It's a concept we have stood behind for three decades and it's working. Since the first day we opened our doors and set out to change the salon experience, an overwhelmingly loyal client base continues to cheer us on.

Our Commitment

We are extremely thankful for the trust our loyal customers have shown us from the beginning. We'd like to earn your trust too. If you are ready for dramatic results without all the drama, give us a call today.
